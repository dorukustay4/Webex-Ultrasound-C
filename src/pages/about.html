About UltraAnnotate Platform

Local annotation platform for ultrasound experts to educate trainees and collaborate with peers through screen sharing

Our Mission

UltraAnnotate is a locally-running annotation platform designed specifically for ultrasound experts to efficiently annotate medical imagery while simultaneously sharing their screens in any web conferencing tool. This enables real-time education of trainees and collaborative consultation with peers.

Our platform eliminates the need for proprietary video conferencing solutions by providing powerful local annotation tools that work seamlessly with your existing communication infrastructure, whether it's Zoom, Teams, Google Meet, or any other screen sharing solution.

Key Features

Local Annotation Tools
Powerful annotation capabilities running entirely on your local machine for optimal performance and privacy
Real-Time Screen Sharing
Share your annotated ultrasound images instantly through any web conferencing platform of your choice
Universal Compatibility
Works with Zoom, Teams, Google Meet, or any screen sharing solution - no proprietary video platform required
Session Library
Local storage of annotations and sessions for easy reference, teaching materials, and case studies
Expert-Designed Interface
Purpose-built for medical professionals with intuitive tools for efficient ultrasound annotation workflows
Educational Focus
Specialized features for training scenarios, peer consultation, and knowledge transfer in medical settings
